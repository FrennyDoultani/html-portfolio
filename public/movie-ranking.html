Five best movies according to Frenny

Favorite movies of all time

1. Yeh jawani hai deewani

Because there is the friends group in the movie that never gets seperated and also a cute love story.

2. Zindagi naa milegi doobara

Because it is the movie that give us the lesson that we should live our life on our own terms and without getting worried about the future we should live our today.

3. Jab we met

Because it has a love story in which girl changes boy's thinking in terms of life and teaches him to live the life as the best way possible

4. Hum saath saath hai

Because it has the big joint family that lives together happily even after the challenges they face

5. 3 idiots

Because it teaches us that apart from studies and career tension we should socialize ourselves and also choose the career path that makes us the happiest, we should make our parents understand that we can do our best in anything if and only if we are happy in that task.
